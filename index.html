Joey's Journal

Joey is an eight-year-old black and white domestic shorthair cat, classified
as a “tuxedo.” A big and strong 15 pounds, Joey enjoys eating delicious
treats and playing in the backyard.


How It Started

Born in the backyard of his adopted-family’s relative, with a brother and a sister,
Joey didn’t always live a life of luxury. When he was just a few months old, the
Levinsons took him in and accepted him as one of their own, spoiling him with a life
of comfy beds and unlimited food. Upon taking him home, it was a struggle for the
Levinsons to choose his name. It had to fit his personality and his looks. It had to be
him. Oreo? Ninja? None of it seemed quite right, but when he playfully stood on his tiny
hind legs like a baby kangaroo, they knew Joey was the purrfect name for him.
